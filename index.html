Costa Rica

Winter is coming, which, for those of us living in the Pacific Northwest, means short days, dark clouds, rain, freezing temperatures, and the longing for spring to return.

It's usually around the new year that I start to crave warm, sandy beaches, sunny skies, clear blue water, tropical drinks and a sunburn. This year, Costa Rica is on top of my list of tropical paradises I'd like to migrate south to.
